Simon -
The Interactive Memory Game

How to play:

1- When the game starts Simon will play a tune and the lights will flash in sequence, this signals the game has started.

2- The level indicator in the middle will change to 01 an Simon will light up one colour.

3- You must click on the same colour to progress.

4- Simon will then add another colour to the previous sequence.

5- You will repeat this sequence by clicking the colours in the correct order.

6- Each time you get a sequence correct the levels will go up and Simon will continue to add more and more colours to the sequence.

7- The game will end when you incorrectly guess the sequence. The is indicated by all the colours flashing and the game over sound.

8- Press Reset to start a new game.

9- Try to Beat your High Score!!

10- Press PLAY GAME to begin, Have Fun!!
